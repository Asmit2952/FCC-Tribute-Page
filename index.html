A Timeline on Pewds:

  • Born on 24th October, 1989 in Sweden
  • Pursued a degree in industrial economics and technology management at Chalmers Univeristy of Technology
  • Registered an account on YouTube under the name PewDiePie
  • Dropped out of college as he got bored with his studies
  • Failed to get an apprenticeship at an advertising firm, started doing YouTube full time
  • Started gaining popularity with Let's Plays of games like Amnesia
  • Surpassed 1 million subscribers in 2012
  • Most subscribed YouTube channel since August, 2013
  • 109 million subscribers as of July, 2021
  • Developed various games like Legend of the Brofist and Tuber Simulator
  • Made a show on YouTube Red called "Scare PewDiePie" starring various YouTubers
  • Got caught up in controversy as he was accused of being an anti-semite by the WSJ in February, 2017
  • Got married to Marzia on 19th August, 2019
  • Currently living in Brighton, England with his wife Marzia
